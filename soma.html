UI & UX Challenge(s)

There are a few key pieces of information in the HUD presented to the player:
(1.) Camera Yaw (Horizontal) Rotation
(2.) Camera Pitch (Vertical) Angle
(3.) Camera Zoom Factor
(4.) Audio bars (visualizer)
I wanted to find a way to present all of this without obscuring the player’s view, particularly the center of the screen.

The inspiration for the zoom was taken from a screen in a vehicle from Cyberpunk 2077. I placed it in the corner to avoid blocking the center of the camera. Same goes for the camera rotations (horizontal + vertical).

Retrospective

Upon user testing of the UI in the engine, the testers mostly ignored the UI and instead rotated the camera and didn't know you could zoom in - there were no button prompts. The zoom visuals got the most attention out of the information.
